SR2023 Competition Dates Announced
The SR2023 competition will be held on the weekend of the 1st and 2nd of April 2023. We're working hard on confirming the venue but hope that you can at least mark the date in your calendars!

December Virtual Tech Day
Unfortunately due to circumstances outside of our control, we had to pivot the December Tech Day to be virtual. We instead held it on our Discord server with volunteers on hand to help with any issues or problems the competitors had.
December Social
We had a volunteer social on Saturday 10th December. It was a lot of fun with chatting and some casual party/group games. Keep an eye on #social in Slack to see the planning for our next one. All volunteers are most welcome :)
