2015-01-28 : SR2015 Issue 14
This is the fourteenth issue of the Student Robotics (Almost) Weekly Newsletter, a (roughly) fortnightly newsletter which summarises the goings‚Äêon across Student Robotics. Emboldened items are ones which we believe could do with extra hands. An absence of emboldening does not necessarily mean that everything is done.
Internal updates:
  • Mentoring in Southampton has started, a bit later than usually though.
  • The first Tech Day in Munich happened at MAI, who have built an arena for testing.
  • Competition Software now runs on Python 3 and uses Setuptools for installation.
  • SRBot has been replaced by Willie.
  • Sign-ups for a committee position have now closed.
  • Various updates to Southampton venue ideas for the competition have happened.
  • There is a date hold on Newbury Racecourse as a competition venue.
  • The first Volunteer Activity Day is happening this week.
  • A summary of the possible results from the committee vote has been posted.
